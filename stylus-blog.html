Stylus Marketing

My official first internship at stylus I learnt alot (alot alot), from alot of industry proffesionals and met alot of smart and talented people.

Working onsite in Toronto while commuting was amazing I loved waking up every day not knowing what was going to happen at work, one day Id be meeting with clients another day I would attend meetings with managers and big clients that needed software, everyday was different however very productive.

At Stylus, I worked on fullstack development, I was given an important role of coordinating my development team and making sure everyone was doing their part which allowed to gain some very valuable leadership skills.

Not only that I was conducting meetings and acting as lead developer in major meetings where there were high stakes for the company.

All in all I was able to create an impact at Stylus and implement my technical skills learnt throughout my years of school in the real world which is what matters the most to me!

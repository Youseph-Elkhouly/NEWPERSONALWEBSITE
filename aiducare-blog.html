Aiducare

Aiducare was my first remote internship experience, communicating was more of a struggle as I had to booking meetings and ensure that my development process was well understood by my team, However it was great, I devlivered and met great people.

The onboarding process was simple and they layed out my job and requirements in a simple way

Not only was I not comfortable with react native, but I had to find a way to demonstrate my work, through expogo testing. These new techonologies I picked up quick and began developing my own features and components using them.

The remote work environment at Aiducare wasnt my favorite, so I constantly found ways to work more efficent by going to coffee shops and constantly changing my environment to avoid burnout.

In the end I delivered a working product that the aiducare team was happy with and is still working on. So I am happy that I was able to contribute efficently to the team and help them improve their product.
